Privacy Policy

Your data is yours. We don't store it. We don't share it. We don't need it.

No Data Storage

HALONEX does not store, collect, or process any personal or financial data. The ring is a payment token — it contains no user-identifiable information. Your payment credentials are securely managed by your bank.

Payment Tokenization

When you activate your HALONEX ring, a digital token is provisioned to the ring via Digiseq (a UK-based TSM). This token is unique to your ring and cannot be used without your bank's authorization. No payment data is ever stored on the ring itself.

Bank-Grade Security

HALONEX uses the same Visa and Mastercard secure infrastructure as your physical payment card. All transactions are encrypted and processed through your bank's existing systems. HALONEX never sees, stores, or transmits your payment details.

No Tracking

HALONEX does not track your location, spending habits, or usage patterns. The ring is a passive device — it only activates when you tap it to a payment terminal. There is no connectivity, no Bluetooth, no Wi-Fi, no GPS.

Your Bank, Your Data

Your payment data remains exclusively with your bank. HALONEX is simply the physical token that carries your bank's authorization. We have no access to your transaction history, account balance, or personal information.

Learn More

If you have questions about how your payment data is handled, please contact your bank directly. For inquiries about HALONEX, reach out to us at info@halonex.uk.
